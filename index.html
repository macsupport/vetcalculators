About Vetcalculators

Vetcalculators.com was designed to help Veterinarians easily calculate dosages of common anesthetic, emergency and pain drugs for their feline and canine patients. It is solely a guide and each dose should be thouroughly checked for accuracy and the specific dose given should be based on the Veterinarian's current physical exam as well as the patients age, medical history and current health status. Vetcalculators.com is not responsible for any errors in dosing based on this calculator.
